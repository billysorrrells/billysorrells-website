The Story

From Houston
To Hollywood

Billy Sorrells is a stand-up comedian, actor, and host. Starting his career in the digital space, Billy produced and starred in sketches with his character “Peaches” which grew to over 17 million views. He created and starred in Office Code for BET, hosted Red Carpet coverage for BET Awards, and appeared in Black Jesus (Adult Swim), Laff Mobb’s Laff Tracks (TruTV), and Black Card Revoked (BET).

Cast member on MTV’s Wild ’N Out (Seasons 8 & 14), series regular on Funniest Wins (TBS), and featured in his own half-hour special for Kevin Hart’s LOL Network. He had a cameo in Travis Scott’s “Sicko Mode” video (448M+ views) and Yo Gotti’s “Down in the DM” (136M+ views).

“Using the wisdom of his grandfather as a guide, Billy turned Houston comedy into a worldwide movement.”

His 2024 comedy special “Toxic AF” streams on Amazon, Apple TV, and Comedy Dynamics. Today, Billy is the founder of Dirty Sexy Funny, Houston’s premier comedy series streaming on Tubi, Apple TV, and Fox Soul. Represented by Innovative Artists, Los Angeles.
