About Billy

"The Sexiest Man in Comedy"

Billy Sorrells is a force in entertainment—a comedian, actor, writer, and producer who has built an empire from the stage to the screen. A veteran of MTV's Wild N Out (Seasons 8 & 14+), Billy's razor-sharp wit and undeniable charisma have made him one of the most sought-after entertainers in the game.

With over 110,000 YouTube subscribers, 17M+ views, and a comedy special Toxic AF streaming everywhere, Billy brings the energy whether he's headlining sold-out shows, producing original content, or developing his slate of TV and film projects through BSE LLC.

From Katy, Texas to stages across the country, Billy Sorrells doesn't just do comedy—he commands it.
