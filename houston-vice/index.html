The Power Network

The antagonist structure of HOUSTON VICE mirrors real Houston power dynamics: cartel money flowing through the Port, laundered through real estate development, protected by corrupt officers inside HPD. This isn't one villain — it's a system. Dom Cruz is the visible face. The Businessman is the money. The Protected Officer is the shield. And the cartel is the engine underneath it all.
